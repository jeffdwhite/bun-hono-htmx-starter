HTMX Integration

HTMX enables rich interactions without writing JavaScript.

Common Patterns

Debounced Search

<input
  hx-get="/search"
  hx-trigger="keyup changed delay:300ms"
  hx-target="#results"
/>

Form Submission

<form
  hx-post="/items"
  hx-target="#content"
>

Partial vs Full Page

Use renderResponse() to detect HTMX requests and return partials or full pages.
